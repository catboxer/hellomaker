Rebranding

Yanker rompers were initially designed as a fastener free outfit for infants. As Yankers began to move into the adult market, negative feelings around the name Yanker came to light during product validation interviews. Therefore the decision was made to rebrand. In 2007 Yanker was created as an alternative to fastening and unfastening an infant romper 8 to 10 times a day. It was designed as the rational alternative to dressing babies.

I looked into the history of the Union Suit and focused in on the dress reformers from the late 1800s. They believed that no one should have to wear clothing that was uncomfortable, impeded movement or caused physical harm. Because the brand already had a message of feminist empowerment it seemed a natural direction to go. The dress reformers called themselves the Rational Dress Society. Yanker decided to sell the one piece Union Suit that did not require women to undress when using the bathroom under the name Rational Clothing

Shopify

Rational Clothing is currently being built out on the Shopify platform with many customized features. One of the most important aspects of this brand are their sustainable fabrics. Using the Shopify metafield objects I built out tabs that dynamically generated fabric information & sustainability efforts depending on the variation selected.

Dynamically Generated Tab Content

Breaking up the content in this way allows Rational to go into depth about each fabric without overwhelming the customer.

Dynamically Generated Size Chart

Dynamically generated size tabs determined by the product tag allow customers to make sure they are getting a proper fit and therefore there are less returns.

Customized FAQ Page

An FAQ that opens up the answers only when clicked limiting content to what the user needs. An easy to read FAQ means customers require less customer service saving time and resources. An easy to fill out form in the backend of Shopify allows store editors to easily add new and rearrange questions and topics to the list.

Customized Review App


An integrated and customized review app makes sure each product gains more SEO points and provide users trust around their purchases.
