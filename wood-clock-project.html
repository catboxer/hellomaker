Peter Finch is a gifted woodworker, sought after by artesian restaurants in San Francisco.

Portfolio Website

Peter has a huge body of work that needs to be conveyed without overwhelming the reader. I am in the process of building out a simple portfolio website with interactive cards that detail some of his projects. I am making it visually fun to interact with his projects by adding motion to a simple scroll layout so that the projects can be glanced at quickly. The front page is a (fun!) placeholder. I am waiting on him to finalize his branding before moving forward.
