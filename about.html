Who Am I?

I've been in your shoes. I supported my own family for 15 years+ running my own E-commerce business.

Over the years I've had the opportunity to use various e-commerce platforms including Magento, WordPress, Shopify and BigCommerce.
‍
Because I've played on both sides of the board I come with the unique vantage point of knowing firsthand your needs and can better match you up with the right solution for your business goals.

I've spent a lot of time working with contractors and understand the hangups that can happen along the way. One of my first jobs out of business school was as a product development manager working with a team of 8 developers in London. It taught me how paramount good communication is to a successful project. My system is set up to help mitigate misunderstandings through clear and effective communication and documentation.

Over the past decade I have come to find my passion lies around creating brands and websites. I take the time to get to know you and what makes you excited. I then transcribe your essence into a website.

My goal is to have every client leaving with a website they want to share with the world.

You are responsible for a lot of moving parts. Your website is just one of them. Contact me with your website vision to see how I can help you get to where you want to be.

My focus is working with creative people who are passionate about their craft or brands creating products that have a social message.
